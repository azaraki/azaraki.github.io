I am a Senior Lecturer in Robotics and AI at the University of Hertfordshire, with extensive experience in artificial intelligence, robotics, and human-robot interaction. My research focuses on developing intelligent systems that can adapt and interact naturally with humans, particularly in the areas of bio-inspired attention systems, reinforcement learning for robotic control, and assistive technologies for children with autism.

My work spans multiple domains including bio-inspired attention systems, reinforcement learning for robotic control, and assistive technologies for children with autism. I have published extensively in top-tier journals and conferences, contributing to both theoretical advances and practical applications in the field of robotics and AI.
