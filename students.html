Supervision Philosophy

My approach to student supervision is based on fostering independence, critical thinking, and research excellence while providing the support and guidance needed for success.

Individualized Guidance

Each student has unique strengths, interests, and career goals. I tailor my supervision approach to match individual needs and learning styles.

Research Independence

I encourage students to develop their own research ideas and take ownership of their projects while providing guidance and support when needed.

Collaborative Environment

I foster a collaborative research environment where students can learn from each other and participate in broader research discussions.

Professional Development

Beyond research skills, I help students develop professional competencies including writing, presentation, and networking skills.
