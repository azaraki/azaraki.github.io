ARI humanoid robot imitates human gaze behaviour using reinforcement learning in real-world environments

K. Ghamati, A. Zaraki, F. Amirabdullahian
IEEE-RAS 23rd International Conference on Humanoid Robots (Humanoids)
2024, France
View on Google Scholar Download PDF

Abstract

This research contributes to the field of humanoid robot by investigating ari humanoid robot imitates human gaze behaviour using reinforcement learning in real-world environments. The work presents novel approaches and methodologies that advance our understanding of human-robot interaction and artificial intelligence applications.
